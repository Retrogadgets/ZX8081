To get into the background, I pulled out a run of 10 clones, which gave me enough problems, although thanks to the help of my colleagues who stayed behind (especially José Leandro who managed to make my badge run) I have an operative clone in my possession.

Once the errors are found, I will take a second run with the corrected errors. I do not want to make an extensive run in case some more unforeseen error appears (you know that whenever you play you can make mistakes), but in principle you should not.

The routing is improved by José Leandro, and has chosen to integrate a membrane keyboard that would mount in China, just as the original. 
This last keyboard theme has become more expensive than initially planned, so the final price is € 65. 

In case people see it very expensive and there are not enough people to complete the quota, we can choose to manually build the keyboard because it was the initially planned option and the clone would have a final cost of € 37. 

In principle everything is already clear and corrected, although whenever you touch you can incur an error, so the current print will limit it to 20 units and if it is confirmed that everything is OK I will get a bigger roll.

People who paid for their badge and have not received their kit can choose between paying the difference or asking for the money back. 

The approach is as follows: 

- The expected cost of each plate with integrated keyboard and its components is about € 65. I will try to check all the components on my plate before doing the shipping to make sure that they all work. 
- The cost of the individual plate with the built-in membrane keyboard is € 30. 
- In both cases, the shipment must be added. 
- I think all the costs are pretty well calculated, unless they put me a stick not provided in the customs.
- I do not have the capacity to advance the cost of 20 units (€ 1300) so I have no choice but to ask for the money in advance once the circulation is confirmed and again confirm the budgets with the Chinese. If afterwards it is cheaper or more expensive, we will make accounts. 
- The box is not included in the price. Those who want it, you can manifest it in this thread or get in touch with alvaroalea. The price is € 32 + shipping 

The price is the kit without mounting, but if someone does not have the ability to mount it, I can mount it for the same price to not increase the cost of the clone. Please bear in mind that it is a long setup, so I ask you to only opt for this option if you do not dare to do the editing for yourselves.
